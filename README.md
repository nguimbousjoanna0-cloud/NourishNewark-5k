# Nourish Newark 5K — Official Registration Portal

**We Fuel the City — Powered by Real Food**

This repository contains the official registration and payment landing page for the Nourish Newark 5K. This community visibility movement is focused on health, community awareness, and authenticity.

## 🗓️ Event Details
- **Date:** Saturday, May 24, 2026
- **Time:** 9:30 AM – 11:00 AM
- **Location:** Newark, NJ
- **Registration Fee:** $10

## 👟 How it Works
1. **Register:** Fill out the participant form on the live site.
2. **Pay:** Complete the $10 registration fee via Zelle or PayPal.
3. **Run:** Join us at the starting line at 545 Central Avenue!
